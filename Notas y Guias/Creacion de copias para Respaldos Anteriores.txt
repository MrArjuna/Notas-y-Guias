-Crear Instantaneas

vssadmin create shadow /For=C:




Lista de Instantaneas


vssadmin list shadows



-Eliminar Instantanea


vssadmin delete shadows /shadow={ID-de-la-copia}


-Eliminar la ultima (PowerShell).

$oldestShadow = vssadmin list shadows /for=C: | Where-Object { $_ -match "ID: {.*}" } | Select-Object -First 1
